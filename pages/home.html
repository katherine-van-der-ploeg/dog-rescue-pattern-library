About Us

Who we are.

At Loyal Rescue, we are dedicated to the animals that come into our care. When they ar-rive we work around the clock to get them the care and medical attention they require before they are placed in their foster homes.There, they are cared for like family until their forever home comes along. This is where you come in. The 20 minute long application must be filled out before you can visit with any of our animals and for good reason. Only a family that is as dedicated to their future friend as we are is going to be selected and if this pup is not a good fit, we promise we will find you one that is.

Our Application Process

Yes, we know that our adoption application is a long one. We did that on purpose. Not only does our application tell us everything about you personally, but it also gives us an idea of what kind of pet-parent you will be. At Loyal Rescue, we are incredibly dedicated to finding the best homes for our animals. Based on your application we will decide who your best match might be and will put you in contact with the foster family that is currently caring for your new friend. From there you may schedule as many meetings as you like to make your final decision.
